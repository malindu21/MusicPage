About Me

Hi, I'm Malindu Kirindegamaarachchi.

I'm a singer who focuses on creating quality music with some of Sri Lanka's most respected producers and lyricists. My journey started with "Saavi" (2017), produced by Iraj, and my breakthrough came with "Kathirina" (2020) which has reached over 14 million views - the most famous song that I gave my voice to.

I prefer to let my music speak for itself. I work quietly, choose my collaborations carefully, and always prioritize quality over quantity. When I'm not making music, you'll find me coding.

Thank you for listening.
